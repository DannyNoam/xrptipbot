How does it work?

Login

Simply login using the 'Login' button in the top-right of the screen. You'll be prompted to authenticate using Discord's secure authentication endpoint.

Deposit

Simply use your CasinoCoin wallet to deposit funds to the Tip Bot wallet. Don't forget to enter the destination tag!

Activate the bot

With both Discord and Twitter currently supported, simply activate the bot using commands such as: Hey @persontotip, here's +4 @CSCTipBot.

Withdraw

Been tipped? Or simply want to withdraw? Go to the withdraw page, and enter your wallet details. It's as simple as that.
